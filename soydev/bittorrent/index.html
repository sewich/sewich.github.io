Networking setup

Set global upload and download limits that doesn’t use the whole bandwidth of your device or router.

If you don’t have rj45 connection and experience problems with internet despite upload limits, lower max. number of connections, upload slots and switch protocol to TCP only. If it still doesn’t help, then you have proven that wi-fi isn’t as good as the companies try to sell it to you. Connect your device over ethernet for better connection and bandwidth.

ISP blocking

Most ISPs don’t care. Some will block torrenting, you should contact them if you’re sure it happens. You can check laws in each country that try restrict file sharing. You shouldn’t stop even if you receive e-mails threatening you if you never experienced an ISP cancelling you, because 99% of the time they’re just trying to scare you. If you experienced censorship from your ISP, you can try torrenting through a reputable VPN.
